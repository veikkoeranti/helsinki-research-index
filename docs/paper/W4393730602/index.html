Abstract

(Very) simple 3D model of the Suomenlinna sea fortress off of the coast of Helsinki, Finnland. Topographic data from the Geological Survey of Finnland; building footprints from Open Street Map. The building extrusion is simple and there are some errors (holes) but I have already spend too much time on this... Source: Objaverse 1.0 / Sketchfab

Confidence notesThis is a technical 3D modeling project of Suomenlinna, a UNESCO World Heritage site located off Helsinki's coast. It is not an academic paper analyzing urban phenomena, but rather a technical documentation of a 3D model. No specific Helsinki neighbourhoods are discussed as case sites. Included because Suomenlinna is administratively part of Helsinki and the work explicitly references Helsinki in its context.
