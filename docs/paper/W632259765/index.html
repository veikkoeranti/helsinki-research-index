1996 · Marja Heinonen · Safety Warnings and Signage

CYCLISTS' AND MOTORISTS' KNOWLEDGE OF CYCLING-RELATED TRAFFIC RULES BEFORE AND AFTER THE "MALMI" CAMPAIGN

OpenAlex · en

Abstract

The city of Helsinki organised a local campaign in the Malmi district of the city during the spring and summer of 1995. The campaign involved various actions such as local press publicity and special street arrangements designed to improve cycling safety. The task of the Central Organization for Traffic Safety in Finland was to determine cyclists' and motorists' knowledge of cycling-related traffic rules before and after the campaign. Both cyclists and motorists had a poor knowledge of the give-way rules.
