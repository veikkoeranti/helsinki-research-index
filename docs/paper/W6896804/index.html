2007 · Tuomo Hiippala · Discourse Analysis in Language Studies

Helsinki: A Multisemiotic Analysis of Tourist Brochures

OpenAlex · en

Abstract

This thesis analyses tourist brochures as a genre of print media, focusing on the city of Helsinki, the capital of Finland.Drawing on the systemic-functional theory of language by Halliday (1978Halliday ( , 1994)), the study conducts a multisemiotic discourse analysis of the collected materials, focusing on language, image, colour, composition and typography.The analysis attempts to show how both content and appearance of the brochures have changed, and which factors have motivated the change in the representation of Helsinki.
