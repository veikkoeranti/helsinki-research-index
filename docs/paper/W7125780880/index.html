Balancing sustainable Mobility and Safety – how to fit e-scooters into urban transport, case Helsinki

OpenAlex · DOI

Abstract

This study aimed to analyze the current state of shared e-scooter parking and potential parking-related challenges to address in the Kallio district in Helsinki, Finland. In addition, the current status of e-scooter rules and restrictions was collected.
