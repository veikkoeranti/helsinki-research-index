1997 · A Huhdanmaki · Transportation Planning and Optimization

IMPACTS OF TRAFFIC PLANS ON URBAN FORM IN HELSINKI METROPOLITAN AREA

OpenAlex · en

Abstract

The main objective of the study is to develop concrete tools for evaluating the impacts of traffic plans and systems on urban form in the Helsinki metropolitan area. Particular attention is paid to evaluating tools likely to be the most successful in integrating land use and transportation planning. The second object of the study is to evaluate tentatively what impacts earlier traffic plans and system decisions have had on the urban structure in the Helsinki metropolitan area in recent decades.
