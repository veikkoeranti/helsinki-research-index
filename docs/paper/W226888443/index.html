Titanium White Knights, Helsinki

OpenAlex · en

Abstract

In the front of Helsinki's south harbour there is a group of four islands that make up the UNESCO World Heritage-listed sea fortress of Suomenlinna, and that is where I'm writing this article. This is an island group of contrasts as it caters for prisoners (a Finnish Alcatraz) and tourists, and houses the military as well as artists.
