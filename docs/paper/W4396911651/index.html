Youth in the media city

OpenAlex · DOI · en

Abstract

Back cover text Welcome to the journey of two media cities, Helsinki and Saint Petersburg, to explore young people’s lives and cultures on the move in these environments and landscapes! As a reader, you may dive into partly hidden urban youth subcultures: encounters in an urban circus, active Pokémon Go player communities, sticker artists redefning the city, or debating gender in grafti subculture. Take a look at just one essay or read them all. Or you may want to focus solely on the ponderable photos, or drift away by listening to the mesmerizing metro sounds in the digital version of this book, available here: www.youth-in-the-mediacity.org.
