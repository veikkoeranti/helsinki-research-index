Functions of Usage of Urban Place Names

OpenAlex · en

Abstract

The following paper introduces various functions of urban place names by analyzing spoken data gathered&#13;\nfrom residents in the neighbourhood Kallio in Helsinki, Finland. The data consists of six group interviews,&#13;\nin which groups with two to six members answer questions concerning their home neighbourhood.&#13;\nI have studied various kinds of place names (both official and unofficial), which the inhabitants of&#13;\nKallio use daily, in terms of how the age, range, and users of the names vary, and what types of images the&#13;\ninhabitants have about the names. On the basis of this, I introduce four functions of urban place names: the&#13;\ncollective, practical, affective and informative functions.&#13;\nBesides the functions, I also studied another theme – that of the roles of the name users. To mention&#13;\nbut a few, the roles include those of the name knower, name specialist, name giver and name learner.
