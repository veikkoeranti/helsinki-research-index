Click to increase image sizeClick to decrease image size Additional informationNotes on contributorsDeana McDonaghBiographyDr Deana McDonagh is an Associate Professor of Industrial Design in the School of Art + Design and the Beckman Institute of Advanced Science and Technology at the University of Illinois (Urbana-Champaign), USA. Her previous academic position was as a Reader in User-centred Industrial Design in the Department of Design and Technology at Loughborough University (UK). She focuses on developing empathic design research methods to elicit user needs beyond the functional. Her current work concentrates on integrating the marginalized voice within the designing process (for example, community members, people with physical disabilities and elders). She is a Fellow of the World Demographic Association and the Design Research Society.
